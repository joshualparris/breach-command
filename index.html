How to Play

Goal. Push through the sector. Survive the final breach.

Turn flow. Each turn your three units can move and take one action. Enemies show their intent before you commit. End your turn to resolve theirs.

Controls. Tap a unit to select. Highlighted tiles show moves. Tap an ability, then a valid target. Undo reverts the current turn. Tap enemies to inspect intent.

Push & pull. Force movement into walls, pits, or spikes is extremely strong. Use it.

Hazards. Pits drop units out. Spikes hurt on entry. Vents push any unit that ends a turn on them.

Between battles. Pick upgrades, repair at rest stops, and plan your path. Elites hit harder but pay more.
